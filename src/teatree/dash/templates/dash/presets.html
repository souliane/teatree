Preset entries

A preset holds one of three opinions per loop. on forces the loop to run, off masks it, and no opinion hands the decision to the loop's own base setting — now and in future, so flipping that base later silently changes what this preset does. Presets do not inherit from one another; resolution is hold → forced override → preset → base.
